Store Wallet:

This wallet stores all the payments you receive for orders fulfilled via Refr Cash and online transactions

You can transfer this money to your Campaign Wallet to keep your campaign running

Payouts to your bank account will happen every 14 days

To be credited: This section shows payments of orders with ongoing return period. The amount gets credited to your Store Wallet post return period completion


Campaign Wallet:

Your campaign rewards are given out via this wallet

Keep it fueled at all times so you get maximum visibility on the Refr marketplace

You can either Add funds directly to this wallet or transfer funds from your Campaign Wallet
