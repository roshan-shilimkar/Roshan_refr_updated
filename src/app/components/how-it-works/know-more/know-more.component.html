Refr Cash is the in-app currency. All the cashback you receive on Refr is in the form of Refr Cash. This is available in your Refr wallet. You can use this Refr cash for payments to all stores listed on the Refr Marketplace or you can withdraw it to your linked UPI wallets.

The value of 1 Refr Cash is equal to 1 rupee! Which means if you have 1500.Refr Cash, you have equivalent to 1500 rupees & you can use all of it on any store listed on Refr on a single purchase without any cap on spending…unbelievably true right!

Where can you spend Refr cash?

You can spend your collected Refr cash across all categories & stores listed on Refr. This means you can spend the cashback earned from a restaurant on your next haircut at your favourite salon, making that haircut absolutely free!!

How to withdraw Refr cash?

You can withdraw your Refr cash to your linked UPI wallets. Minimal withdrawal amount is 1000 Refr cash. On every withdrawal, a charge of 20% will be levied.

Validity of Refr cash?

Your Refr cash will be valid as long as you are a part of Refr.

How to redeem Refr cash on offline purchases?

After recommending the product or service post purchase by scanning the store’s QR code, simply click ‘Pay & Redeem’ to redeem your cashback. The merchant will approve your request and you will be credited with the cashback instantly. This will reflect in your Refr wallet & can be used to make any future purchases on Refr.

How is Refr cash credited for online stores?

For all online purchases made on Refr, cashback will auto-reflect in the ‘To be credited’ section in your Refr wallet. This amount will not be usable until the product is delivered and accepted. Once you have accepted the order, your cashback will get auto-transferred to your ‘Available Balance’ section, which is usable Refr cash that can be used on subsequent purchases on Refr.

What happens to my cashback if I return an online order?

If you happen to return an online order, your cashback will be transferred back to the store from the ‘To be credited’ section in your Refr wallet. You will not be eligible to use this Refr cash.

Refr Levels

Refr levels are a gamification feature that offer you surprise rewards and cashbacks as you reach new stages of progress.

These are based on the following factors:

  • Businesses shared by you – total number of businesses you have shared on Refr.
  • Total earnings – total amount of cashback you have received by recommending businesses and using recommendations from friends.
  • Recommendations used by friends – this indicates how many friends have used your recommendations while making a purchase.
